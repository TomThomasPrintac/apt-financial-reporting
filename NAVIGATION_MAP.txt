================================================================================
                          NAVIGATION BAR BEHAVIOR
================================================================================

Every Panel has consistent navigation at the top:

    [בית]  [Content]  [חזור]  [יציאה]
    Home               Back    Logout

Actions:
• בית (Home):   Returns to appropriate home panel for logged-in role
• חזור (Back):  Returns to appropriate home panel for logged-in role
• יציאה (Logout): Returns to LoginPanel (requires re-login)

Anchor Settings:
• Home button:   Fixed at left (no anchor)
• Back button:   Fixed at right (Anchor = Top | Right)
• Logout button: Fixed at right (Anchor = Top | Right)
